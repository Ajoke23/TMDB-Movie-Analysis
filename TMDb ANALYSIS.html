Conclusions¶

From the analysis¶

  • Action movies made the highest profit across all genres of movies.
  • 'Steven Spielberg' is the director with most movies that made high profit.
  • In year,2015,the highest profit was made across all year, in which "Star Wars: The Force Awakens" was the title of the movie.
  • The top five production companies of the high profit movies: are Universal Pictures,Paramount Pictures,Walt Disney Pictures,Twentieth Century Fox Film Corporation,Columbia Pictures.
  • Popularity, production companies, director are criteria to be considered to make high profit
